Solutions FAQ

Services FAQ

Frequently asked questions regarding our tutoring sessions, team training programs, and billing configurations.

Can we roll over unused tutoring hours? +
Tutoring hours included in the Professional Plan must be scheduled within the monthly billing period. Unused hours do not roll over to the next month.
What is the onboarding timeline for enterprise teams? +
Standard enterprise setups are fully active within 48 hours. Custom LTI integrations or branded landing page requirements typically require 5 to 7 business days.
Do you support direct bank invoicing for teams? +
Yes! For team purchases of 5 or more licenses, we support quarterly or annual bank wire invoicing. Contact corporate billing for invoice scheduling.
